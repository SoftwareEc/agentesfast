🗑️ Data Deletion Instructions

Delete Your Data

At Agentes Fast, users may request deletion of their personal data and account information associated with our services.

How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data, please send an email to:

support@agentesfast.com

Include the following information:

  • Your full name
  • Your email or phone number associated with the account
  • A brief request indicating you want your data deleted

Our team will process your request within a reasonable timeframe.

What Data May Be Deleted

  • Account information
  • WhatsApp conversation history
  • Automation data
  • Stored contact information
  • Associated analytics data
